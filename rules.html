Rules for the Bidding Process

To bid on a project, you must purchase "tickets" which are at a fixed value. For example, if the ticket is $500, then to bid $1000 you have to purchase two of them. It is important to note that all bids submitted go directly to CHEO.

There are 26 project tiles, 4 trades tiles and 6 Chance tiles.

The highest contributor by a participant on a project will have their company name associated with that tile. In addition, the projects that receive the most donations in its name will be placed closer to the usual "Broadway" spot.

Trades can also bid on the trades tiles which will be named the respective utility serviced by each of the top four bidders.

The names put on the Chance tiles will be drawn at the end. To increase your chances, you can purchase more of these cheaper tickets.

In addition, you can bid on the Start tile and "send someone to prison" by bidding on the Prison tile.

Everyone who has donated in the name of a Project, Utility, Prison or Start tile will have their name included somewhere on the board, even if they are not the tile winner. Companies who bid on the Chance tile only and are not the tile winner will not be added to the board.
